About

Josh Talks enables AI labs and enterprise teams to train, evaluate, and scale voice technologies that genuinely understand India's linguistic diversity. We collect, curate, and deliver research-grade conversational and multi-speaker voice datasets across Indian languages, accents, and real-world contexts.

Our datasets are built with rigorous human-in-the-loop quality control, full consent compliance, automated PII redaction, and end-to-end per-file auditability — because data that trains production speech models must meet production standards.

Customers include top AI labs and technology companies across the world.

Quality standards

Data you can trust
in production

Four foundational commitments that distinguish research-grade voice data from commodity datasets. These aren't aspirational claims — they're engineering requirements we hold every dataset to.

Grassroots diversity

Collected from real speakers across states, socioeconomic tiers, and dialect regions — exactly where your product will be used, not studio-controlled environments.

Measurable quality

Five-level human-in-the-loop annotation with automated anomaly detection keeps label error rates exceptionally low and consistently tracked.

Ethical by design

Consent workflows that meet global standards, automated PII redaction, and contributor revenue-share models — data provenance you can explain to any regulator.

Enterprise-grade security

Air-gapped labs, ISO-27001-aligned cloud practices, and full per-file audit trails — the compliance paper trail your legal and security teams will ask for.
